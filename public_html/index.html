Trend Track

An open community aimed at tracking trends in the software development market

With Trend Track you can

Follow companies

Follow what technologies companies are using. You can also search which companies are using every programming language and framework.

Search and track job offers

Search for jobs with a search system based on tags. You can combine the tags you want in your searches. You can also follow new offers of jobs available with the tags of interest.

Find people

You can search and find people that have experience in every programming language. You can also see what are the people who are more active on github for each programming language.

And much more!

Contribute to the project in github. If you can't contribute in github so just follow us on social networks and stay informed about the project :D
